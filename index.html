Tourist Guide Dompet Dhuafa Australia, Ltd

Responsible for guiding tourists on pre-planned trips. Their tasks include planning and organizing the trip, providing information about the history, culture, and significance of the places visited, answering tourists' questions, managing travel logistics, handling problems that arise during the trip, and collecting feedback from tourists to improve future tours. A tourist guide should also have excellent communication skills, good knowledge of the area, and a friendly and engaging personality to keep tourists interested and engaged throughout the tour.

Education Mentor Jampang English Village

Jampang English Village (JEV) is program of Zona Madina Dompet Dhuafa to support the development of 'Kampung Inggris Jampang'. JEV is also a community of development program to empower society through English mastery and make Jampang as the center of English teaching and learning in Bogor. JEV has three special programs such as JEV Goes to School, English Protege, and JEV Scholarship.
